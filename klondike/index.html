Klondike

Try to move all cards to the foundations (the 4 empty places at top right).

You can move a card to any of the foundations by clicking the card with the wheel of the mouse (yes, you can click with that wheel; just press it down instead of rolling it). If you wheel-click an area of the game that is not a card, then any card in the game that can be moved to a foundation will be moved there.
